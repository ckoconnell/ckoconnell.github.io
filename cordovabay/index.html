Single Release Promotion - Facebook & Instagram

The single release promotion utilizes the supplied cover art and highlights the release, live tour dates and availability. In keeping with the band's visual aesthetic I've attempted to limit polished elements and have replicated a handheld camera movement. This allows for slower, less "slick" motion graphics while still providing contstant movement which is key is gaining users attention in a quick scrolling situation where you may only have a second to make an impression.

Additionally, still keyframe images have been exported to support the release and allow for reposting of the content while keeping the visuals in the feed unique. The promo visuals have been created with Facebook's reccommended 1:1 ratio, with the key content placed within the safe 9:16 portrait ratio favoured by Instagram. All versions available below.

Contest Campaign - Instagram Stories

Instagram Stories will be leveraged to run a photo-based contest in support of the band's new release and tour. The promotion will consist of two tracks: local and global. The local participants will be tagging their entries with their city of choice for the chance to win two tickets to the show. Global contestants outside of the local areas will be competing for their chance at band merchandise.

Support materials for the contest include an introduction video that would run each time the story is posted, alongside keyframe images with contest instructions and localized information for each upcoming gig (or the global details in that case). Below is an example of the key information and visuals using the Victoria live date.

My #MidnightSurf Photo Contest

  • To enter, take and post a photo as close to midnight as possible.
  • Points for accuracy and awesome-ness.
  • Tag with #MidnightSurf and #Victoria for your chance to win two tickets to the show.
  • Deadline May 6, 2018.
  • Winners Announced May 8, 2018.
