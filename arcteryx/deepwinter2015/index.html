What Does Your Deep Winter Look Like?

How to Enter

To celebrate the Deep Winter Photo Challenge, we invite you to tag your images of what Deep Winter means to you. Share your photos on Instagram or Twitter including the hashtags #DEEPWINTER15 and #ARCTERYX and your photo will show up in our gallery giving you a chance to win some sweet Arc’teryx gear.

Tagged posts will be shown in our Deep Winter gallery (any inappropriate images will be removed ;)

The top 5 photos each month (chosen by our photographers) will be posted for voting by the public. Get the most votes and you WIN an ATOM LT jacket or hoody.

Contest Dates:

  • Contest begins Dec 02, 2014
  • Round one January 2, 2015
  • Round two February 2, 2015
  • Round three March 2, 2015
  • Eligible submissions end March 2, 2015

*No photos will carry over month to month and must be submitted after the 1st of each month to be eligible
