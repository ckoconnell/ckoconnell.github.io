Location Based Layering

In the winter time, we often head to the North Shore Moutains to do a quick mini-tour before work. Here on the coast & at these lower elevations, conditons are often wet & mild. These speedy pre-work excursions never take us above treeline & we are not out in the elements for extended periods of time.

I've tried to make off season trips to South America a regular occurrence, to get some snow time in late summer / early autumn while waiting for winter to come to the northern hemisphere. At this time of year the south is heading into spring. A typical day can involve super warm, spring like conditions at the bottom, a long, slow, frosty chair lift ride, touring in the alpine with high sun exposure but much colder temperatures, gale force winds. The way down of course brings the same range of conditons but in reverse order.
