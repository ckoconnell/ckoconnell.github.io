Location Based Layering

Whether you are ski touring in the Coastal Mountains of British Columbia or the Western Alps of Europe, proper layering is a crucial for regulating body temperature while shielding you from the elements.

The below info will hopefully give you a starting-off point when researching new gear or sorting through your gear closest while packing for your next mission.

The conditions you face differ as you move around the globe in search of fresh powder and new lines. Arc’teryx Product Line Manager, Jonathon Wong provides a bit of advice on layering for ski touring depending on where your search takes you.

Coast Mountains
North America

  • Insulation Layer

    Stikine Jacket

    Very weight efficient, packable piece. The Stikine is great for descents and rest stops. It is an insulated hardshell perfect for stormy conditions with a 2L GORE® outer and DWR treated Thermatek™ insulation that keeps you dry and warm.

  • Base Layer

    Stryka Hoody

    The Stryka has a balaclava style hood with no neck gap that provides some face protection and warmth during stormy conditions. The Stryka is a soft, comfortable piece that can doubles as a light sweater in mountain huts and lodges. The material is performs well as base layer or mid layer.

  • Mid Layer

    Procline Hybrid Hoody

    rocline Hybrid Hoody is a great mid layer that is thermally protective and versatile with good breathability on the skin track up. Great comfort and feel with good packability.

  • Pant

    Lithic Comp

    The Lithic Comp pant is made up of versatile materials and uses a composite build that provides a broad thermal range of comfort in stormy weather conditions. The 3L GORE® Fabric Technology provides wind and water protection during the off lift ride up. The large dump pockets are quick and efficient for touring.

Western Alps
Classic Haute Route

    Alpha Jacket

    Super light weight, and packable weather protection great for year round use. Highly breathable and waterproof using 3L GORE®

  • Insulation Layer

    Nuclei FL

    Weighing in at 280g’s the Nuclei FL offer moisture resistant insulation providing wind and snow protection in light storm conditions.

  • Base Layer

    Phase SL Zip T

    This technical baselayer with the zip neck allows for great ventilation in warmer conditions. The fabric is lightweight with a comfortable next-of-skin feel making it ideal for late season/spring, sunny ski touring.
