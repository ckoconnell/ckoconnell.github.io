Behind The Scenes:
Day 2 ARC'TERYX DEEP WINTER 2016

Photo: Vince Shuley
Photos by: Adam Levitt & Vince Shuley

Day two of the Deep Winter Photo Challenge is complete and the clock ticks into the third and final day. The competing photographers are working against time to score what they hope will be the best photos of the week. With a solid day of good snow and weather behind them, everyone is on high alert and it is anyone's game.

A few of the crews took the Whistler Blackcomb ‘slackcountry’, some with harnesses and drones, taking advantage of the new backcountry rules while the rest opted to continue milking the inbound terrain. Everything was in play including the terrain park, which is not the traditional setting for Deep Winter but plays a big role in the heart of Whistler shredders. The race to the editing room starts tomorrow, the final day of shooting!

Event photographers and long time locals Adam Levitt and Vince Shuley were back at it again stealing the magic moments from behind the scenes. Check more of their shots from today below, and be sure to view the growing event gallery on – Flickr.
