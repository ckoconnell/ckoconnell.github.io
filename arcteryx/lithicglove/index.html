Lithic Glove

A New Level Of Performance

Gloves are one of the most complicated products to build. The hand has a range of motion that enables us to accomplish a wide variety of tasks with precision and dexterity unmatched by any other body part. Constructing a glove that can move with such an unrestricted range of motion is a challenge and requires a level of craftsmanship and pattern making well beyond that of other products we wear.

In 2010 Arc’teryx invented a entirely new way to of constructing waterproof gloves. TiDex™ Glove Technology created a glove with unprecedented levels of dexterity and breathability.

Jump ahead to today and we have taken gloves to the next level. Introducing the 2015 Lithic Glove.

Dexterity

Primaloft™ with Grip Control Technology is designed to minimize slippage between glove layers. This process increases dexterity and grip control by using a resin that stiffens up the insulation strands creating less movement between fabrics.

Fit

Crucial to the functionality to any glove, the Lithic Glove pattern has been created for the intended fit needed for ultimate performance.

Durability/Longevity

N333p-X Face Fabric is a very durable fabric. With the addition of TPU(thermoplastic polyurethane) laminated to the palm and fingers improve durability and grip, protect against abrasions and help enhance grip.

Warmth

Primaloft™ Gold insulation Eco is incredibly compressible with impressive rebound, meaning the insulation is less succeptible packing out over time. This feature provides the continued loft needed for greater warmth. The Face Fabric is made of N333p-X which is highly hydrophobic(repels water) which is laminted to the GORE-TEX®. Dryer gloves = dry hands means greater warmth.
