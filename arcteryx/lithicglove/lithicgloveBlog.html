Lithic Glove

A New Level Of Performance

Gloves are one of the most complicated products to build. The hand has a range of motion that enables us to accomplish a wide variety of tasks with precision and dexterity unmatched by any other body part. Constructing a glove that can move with such an unrestricted range of motion is a challenge and requires a level of craftsmanship and pattern making well beyond that of other products we wear.

In 2010 Arc’teryx invented a entirely new way to of constructing waterproof gloves. TriDex™ Glove Technology created a glove with unprecedented levels of dexterity and breathability.

Jump ahead to today and we have taken gloves to the next level. Introducing the 2015 Lithic Glove.

Dexterity

Supple palm fabrics, targeted selection of Primaloft® insulations and proprietary bonded construction; reduces shear, minimizes resistance to closure and ultimately enhances dexterity.

Fit

A proprietary bonding of layers in conjunction with a low volume palm design, work to complement Arc’teryx TriDex™ Glove Technology for a radically improved fit.

Durability/Longevity

N333p-X Face Fabric is a very durable fabric. The addition of TPU(thermoplastic polyurethane) laminated to the palm and fingers helps to enhance grip and protection against abrasion, improving durability.

Warmth

Hydrophobic, breathable materials, reduce moisture build-up while complementing the Primaloft® insulation package. Unrestricted motion allows for proper circulation and, in conjunction with these materials, gives the Lithic Glove unprecedented warmth to weight ratio and greatly improved packibility.
