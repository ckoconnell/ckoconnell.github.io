The environment we create for ourselves, inside & out, is a reflection of who we are and what we stand for. We believe your outdoor room can and should be an integral part of your living space. A space that not only says something about you but considers the larger environment in which we live and share with other living things.

Let us help you create that unique blend between your vision, function, aesthetics, use of native materials and budget. Our passion for what we do is stamped on all our work and comes with the commitment that, "The only corners we will cut are those to ensure a better fit."

  • The Plan

    If "creative playgrounds" are truly creative, why do they look so similar? Taking time to imagine and design your unique space is a critical part of the process. Whatever happens after is a reflection of this. We love this part!

  • Softscaping

    Plantings are the paints that create the form, line, texture and a sense of enclosure to produce the desired effect. And always with an appreciation and understanding of the importance of ongoing ease of maintenance. Spend your time enjoying life outside.

  • Construction

    Benches, Pergolas, Arbours, Etc.
    Everything must have a function. Our function is to make your space work for you.

  • Maintenance

    A labour of love needs care. We do annual or semi-annual maintenance for our clients.

  • Hardscaping

    Pathways, Driveways, Walls, Etc.
    We want to create as minimal impact as possible in our work. We know it can be done because that's the way we do it.
